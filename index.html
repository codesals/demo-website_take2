Today's Inspirational Story

Unleash your inner elephant!

As a man walked through an elephant camp, he was surprised that these huge creatures were only held back by a little rope tied to their leg, although they could easily break free. The elephant trainer explained:
“When they were very young and much smaller, we used that same size rope to tie them. At that age, it was enough to hold them. As they grow up, they are conditioned to believe the rope can still hold them, so they never try to break free.”
Many of us may go through life believing we cannot do something, simply because we failed at it before.
What belief do you need to break free from today? Which rope is holding you back?
